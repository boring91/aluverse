## Recommendations for Optimizing Marketing Spend

### 1. Maintain Current Hipages Spend

Current performance is strong. No immediate need to increase:

| Scenario | Monthly Spend | Expected Projects | Expected Revenue |
| -------- | ------------- | ----------------- | ---------------- |
| Current  | $677          | 4.6               | $13,096          |
| +25%     | $846          | 5.8               | $16,370          |
| +50%     | $1,015        | 6.9               | $19,644          |

**Recommendation:** Maintain current spend; focus on conversion optimization before scaling.

### 2. Focus on Higher-Value Leads

Current average project: $2,847

Strategies to increase:

- Adjust ad targeting to higher-value suburbs (Sutherland Shire, Eastern Suburbs)
- Emphasize commercial/larger residential in listings
- Minimum project threshold ($2,000+)
- Qualify leads before site visits

**Target:** Increase average to $4,000 (+41%)

### 3. Improve Conversion Rate

Assumed current close rate: 50%

Improvement tactics:

- Faster quote turnaround (same day)
- Follow-up automation (day 3, day 7)
- Professional quote presentation
- Testimonial/portfolio sharing (use P0044, P0048 progress photos)

**Target:** Improve to 65% (+30% more closed deals)

### 4. Geographic Targeting

Based on Sutherland Shire success (P0044, P0047, P0048), consider:

- Targeted local advertising in Sutherland Shire
- Community newsletter ads
- Local Facebook groups
- Referral requests from current clients in area
